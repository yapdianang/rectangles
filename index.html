Concept

Real time object detection, as visualized by self-driving cars.

Demonstration

Illustrates image-detection algorithms bounded by boxes, with information of L2 distance.

Design Philosophy

Alerts were considered to notify users, but rides would be disruptive with pop-ups. We solely prioritize more streamlined notifications.
